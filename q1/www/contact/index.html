Questions?

We have answers.

Q: Did you take the video on the homepage yourself?

A: Yes, I went to Toronto and bought some pretty looking donut from Jelly Modern Donuts and sneak filmed their donut display. The salespeople got suspicious of me because I was in the donut shop for awhile.

Q: What framework did you use for this website?

A: I found a great Bootstrap framework and was guided through by the youtuber who had provided it. Problem was that the creator had a generic template and I had to change many pieces myself. It felt like I was given the tools but the art had to be created.

Q: How long did it take for you to do this assignment?

A: For the entire assignment, it took me 5-6 days. Q4 was the hardest because I had to make it adaptive to different scales and devices, Q4 took me around 4 days and I only teared up once from frustration. Overall it was a great time.
